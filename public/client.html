Liberation

- Abandon 1 city in your hand face down
- Liberate 1 city from your hand face down
- Reveal liberated city simultaneously
- If total unrest in liberated cities is more than player count, smallest front line city falls into chaos
- Use listed powers on your liberated city
- Draw cities or villages until you have at least 3 in hand

Counterattack

- Biggest liberated city falls into chaos, Tsar takes ties
- If front line is empty, game ends

Auction

- Use bonus powers on liberated cities
- Raise bid by any amount of imperial rubles any time
- Withdraw if tied and not last bidder
- When no one wants to, highest untied bidder liberates smallest city on the front line
- Buyer may liberate any cities in chaos

Reaction

- Tsar takes remaining cities in chaos
- Liberated cities go to agenda from lowest to highest

Victory

- Points equals size on liberated and hand cities
- 1 point for rubles
- No points for cities in agenda
- Tiebreaker is biggest city in hand
