Play

- Exile 1 scheme in your hand face down
- Play 1 scheme from your hand face down
- Reveal play scheme simultaneously
- If total time is more than player count, summon left timeline scheme to court
- Take listed actions

Trial

- Draw until you have at least 3 schemes in hand
- Summon highest rank in play to court, ties go to dungeon
- If timeline is empty, game ends:
  - Points equals rank on schemes in hand
  - 1 point for each gold and silver
  - Tiebreaker is highest rank scheme in han

Auction

- Raise bid by any amount of gold at any time
- Withdraw if tied and not last bidder
- When no one wants to, highest untied bidder takes left timeline scheme
- Buyer may take any schemes from court

Verdict

- Imprison lowest rank from court to dungeon
- Reserve schemes in play from lowest to highest
