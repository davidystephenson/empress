Launch

- Scrap 1 ship in your hand face down
- Launch 1 ship into orbit from your hand face down
- Reveal ship simultaneously
- If total time is more than player count, move smallest supply ship to market
- Take listed actions on ship in orbit

Piracy

- Repair until you have at least 3 ships in hand
- Biggest ship in orbit goes to market, ties go to the masses
- If supply is empty, game ends

Auction

- Raise bid by any amount of dollars at any time
- Withdraw if tied and not last bidder
- When no one wants to, highest untied bidder takes smallest supply ship
- Buyer may take any ships from market

Repair

- Give smallest ship in market to masses
- Move ships in orbit to repair from lowest to highest

Victory

- Points equals size on ships in hand and orbit
- 1 point for dollars and credits
- No points for ships in repair 
- Tiebreaker is biggest ship in hand
